Simiko Daal

Simi Ko Daal

This is traditional Nepali food,

commonly found in every kitchen of Nepali Household.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Simi
  • 1 ltr Water
  • 2 tbs Salt
  • 1 tbs Turmeric
  • 2 Medium Sized Green Chillies
  • 1 tbs Jeera [roasted/fried]
  • 3 tbs Garlic Paste or 1 cup Onion(fried)
  • Ginger [as taste]
  • Corriander [as taste]

Steps

  1. Take a Pressure cooker, put 2tbs oil and after heating fry Onion first followed by Jeera and Chillies.
  2. After everything turns brown put Simi and toss for 2-3 times and pour water.
  3. Put Salt, Turmeric and Ginger. If you have Garlic Paste put that.
  4. Cover the pressure cooker with lid and wait for 3-4 whistles.
  5. Now, open the lid and pour some more water as per needed.
  6. Boil the water again and put corriander leaves at last.
  7. Enjoy Simiko Daal with your favorite meal.
