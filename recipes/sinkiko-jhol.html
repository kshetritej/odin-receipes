Sinkiko Jhol

Sinki Ko Jhol

Sinkiko Jhol is common, easiest and fastest

recipe for replacement of veggies.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Sinki
  • 500ml water
  • 1 tbs salt
  • 1/2 tbs turmeric
  • 2 red Chillies, dry
  • 1 tbs jeera

Steps

  1. Take a pan and heat the oil
  2. Fry chillies and jeera
  3. Put Sinkiand toss for 1 minute
  4. Pour water and put turmeric and salt
  5. Wait till the water boils and boil for some more time (2-3 minutes)
  6. Turn off the stove and enjoy the soup with your favorite main meal.
